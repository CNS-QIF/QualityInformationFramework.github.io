Complex Type IfThenEllipticalArcRuleType
Annotations
 
The IfThenEllipticalArcRuleType defines a conditional rule that applies if the feature under consideration is an elliptical arc feature. The rule contains an inherited 'if' part, an inherited ThenPoints part, and two more 'then' parts. The 'then' parts of the rule apply if the 'if' part does not exist or evaluates to true.
