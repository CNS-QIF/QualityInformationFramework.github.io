Complex Type Plane23CoreType
Annotations
 
The Plane23CoreType defines the mathematical core of the geometric entity 'plane_surface(u,v):R2->R3'. Plane is an infinite flat surface. The parametrization of the plane is defined by two vectors: DirU and DirV. The domain values (domainU and domainV) 'limit' the plane infinity.
